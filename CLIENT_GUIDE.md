# Client Guide

Please provide your assets following these specifications:

## Images
- **Hero Image**: 4:5 aspect ratio, max 350KB.
- **Gallery Images**: 4:5 aspect ratio, max 220KB.
- **Couple Images**: 4:5 aspect ratio, max 200KB.

## Text
Fill out the provided `invitation.json` structure or complete the questionnaire with your event details, love story, and quotes.
